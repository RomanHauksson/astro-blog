A [hexaflexagon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flexagon) is a puzzling shape you can fold using a strip of paper. Watch these videos to learn more about them:

## my version

I made a hexaflexagon that's color-coded for easy navigation. Each side corresponds to a primary or secondary color in the [RGB color model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RGB_color_model).

## make your own

1. Print out my template ([A4](/hexaflexagon/template-A4.pdf), [letter](/hexaflexagon/template-letter.pdf)) and cut out the parallelogram. Make sure to leave the white strip on the left intact.
